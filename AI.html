The Impacts of AI

Benefits

AI is a powerful tool that has the power to change and revolutionize our world. Its machine learning algorithms can scan various messages, identify patterns, and make predictions which aid many workers in their jobs around the world. This data driven decision making machine is capable of dishing out accurate information and ultimately makes all tasks miniscule.

Risks

Despite its numerous benefits AI unfortunately gives us many issues to deal with moving forward, an example being job replacement. Automation powered by AI has definitely led to many people losing their jobs. This is due to it eliminating certain routine tasks, and potentially making them obsolete to many companies around the world.

Predictions

Looking ahead, the impact of AI is likely to intensify since it is already influencing many parts of the world. One way it could potentially make things better is by introducing new ways to quickly scan diseases such as cancer and finding cures by combining various chemicals. However, I am also worried about certain privacy risks since the internet by itself is a huge issue and it is hard to say how AI could Influence this. All in all, even though AI has many strongpoints its weaknesses could be a huge hurdle for humanity, so we should navigate this technology with caution.
